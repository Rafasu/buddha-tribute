Siddharta Gautama

The man who founded Buddhism.

Siddharta's Life

  • 490 || 448 BCE - Siddharta Gautama is born.
  • 461 BCE - Around age 29, Gautama had a personal crisis. Realizes he is not inmune from suffering of old age, disease and death.
  • 461 || 460 BCE - Leaves home in search for a solution.
  • 461-455 BCE - Practices Yoga and experiments extreme asceticism, in search for answers.
  • 455 BCE - Becomes Buddha.
